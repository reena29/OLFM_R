Why Online Farmers Market?

Usually farmers markets are open only on certain days of a week and that too for few hours. Because of this time-limit, many potential customers cannot buy fresh produce when they like. Using this website customers can order fresh produce online whenever they want. This may help grow the business of local farmers.

How online Farmers Market works?

1. Farmers can upload and update information about their produce periodically.
2. They can deliver once/twice/thrice a week or daily with/without a delivery charge. Pick up option can be made available.
3. Customers can order fresh produce online from the local farm whenever they want and choose delivery or pick up options.
4. Restaurant owners can also order fresh produce in bulk for which they can get discounted rates.
